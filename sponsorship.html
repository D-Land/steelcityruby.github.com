SPONSORSHIP OPPORTUNITIES

With a heavy focus on creating a welcoming environment, Steel City Ruby has become not only a conference about the technology that we use, but also a conference of the community.

Last year’s success wouldn’t have been possible without the help of our community of sponsors. Steel City Ruby Conference 2012 was sold out months in advance to nearly 300 attendees. We hope to continue that momentum in 2013 to bring in nearly 400 developers and sponsor representatives.
